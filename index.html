Uttar Karnataka

• Uttar Karnataka is the northern region of the state of Karnataka in India.
• It is famous for its rich history, ancient temples, and unique cultural traditions.
• Major districts include Belagavi, Hubballi, Dharwad, Ballari, and Vijayapura.
• The region has important historical monuments like Badami, Aihole, and Pattadakal.
• Kannada is the main language spoken, along with regional dialects.
• Agriculture and industries play an important role in the economy of Uttar Karnataka.
