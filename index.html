Uttar Karnataka

• Northern region of Karnataka, India.
• Known for rich history, ancient temples & vibrant culture.
• Includes Belagavi, Hubballi, Dharwad, Ballari, Vijayapura districts.
• Famous historical monuments: Badami, Aihole, Pattadakal.
• Kannada is the main language with regional dialects.
• Agriculture and industries are important for the economy.
